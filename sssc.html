Sitka Salmon

(March 2024 - March 2025)
  • Project Type: University senior group project
  • Software: Unity, Visual Studio
  • Language: C#

This game was made by myself and two other CS seniors as our capstone project. We worked with the Sitka Sound Science Center (SSSC) to create a game to run on a touchscreen in their gift shop. Players control a salmon as it avoids predators and works its way through streams and ocean waters.
