Sitka Salmon

(March 2024 - March 2025)
  • Project Type: University senior group project
  • Software: Unity, Visual Studio
  • Language: C#

This game was made by myself and two other CS undergrads as our senior capstone project. We worked with the Sitka Sound Science Center (SSSC) to create an educational game to run on a touchscreen in their gift shop. Players control a salmon as it avoids predators and works its way through streams and ocean waters. The game is playable on-site in Sitka, Alaska, and online.
