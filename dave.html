Dave

(Summer 2022)
  • Project Type: individual
  • Software: Unity, VSCode, Clip Studio Paint
  • Language: C#

Dave is a 2D adventure/puzzle game about an office worker experiencing a strange day at work. Advance through levels of the building in search of answers, avoiding obstacles thrown your way by some unseen foe. The game is surrealist, inspired by corporate existential dread and disassociation.
